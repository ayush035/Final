PREDICTION MARKET PLACE :
We offer a prediction market place where you can Bet on existing polls or create one of your own and earn 2% of total bets. 
Polls resolve automatically after the criteria is fulfilled and rewards are disbursed as soon as polls resolve.
Visit https://stogram.xyz/predict

Bet on existing Polls : 
- Select any existing poll to bet on (Make sure the poll is active)
- Click on Place bet button
- Select your preffered option (Yes/No)
- Enter amount of 0g tokens you want to bet
- Place bet
you're all set now, wait for the poll to reach its deadline and resolve if your bet is correct you'll recieve your rewards.

Note: 3% fee applies (2% to poll creator, 1% to platform). Minimum 4% volume required on each side for valid resolution/ otherwise poll
is declared null and void and the 100% of bets placed are returns to the wallets.

Create your own Prediction Poll :

- Click on Create Poll button
- Enter your question about the assets that we support. (Currently only supported for financial markets)
- Select the asset for which you entered the question
- Enter Price Target for the same asset
- Enter a duration when you want the poll to resolve max: 1 Week
- CLick on Create Poll and sign the transaction

Your poll will be created and made live instantly, now you'll rest back while your poll earns you 2% of the bets placed on your poll.
